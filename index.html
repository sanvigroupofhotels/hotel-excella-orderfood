Help & FAQ

How to order? Fill your guest details and select quantities for desired menu items. Then click Confirm Order to send your order to reception via WhatsApp.

Is the menu vegetarian or non-vegetarian? Use the Veg, Non Veg and General tabs to view corresponding items.

How long does delivery take? The estimated delivery is minimum 30 mins and maximum upto 45 mins - we shall try to get you the opted food at the earliest that we can.

Can I modify my order? Yes, update quantities anytime before confirming your order. Once the order is confirmed by you, its considered billed.
